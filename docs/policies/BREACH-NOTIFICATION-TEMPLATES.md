# Breach Notification Templates

Last Updated: January 9, 2026

## Template 1: Individual Notification Letter

```
[Date]

[Individual Name]
[Address]
[City, State ZIP]

RE: Notice of Data Breach

Dear [Individual Name]:

We are writing to inform you of a data breach that may have affected your protected health information (PHI).

**What Happened**
On [date], we discovered that [description of breach]. We immediately took steps to [containment actions].

**What Information Was Involved**
The following types of information may have been affected:
- [List specific data elements: names, dates of birth, medical record numbers, diagnoses, etc.]

**What We Are Doing**
We have:
- [List response actions taken]
- Notified law enforcement [if applicable]
- Implemented additional security measures

**What You Can Do**
We recommend you:
- Monitor your medical records for any unauthorized activity
- Review your insurance Explanation of Benefits statements
- Consider placing a fraud alert on your credit file
- Report any suspicious activity to us immediately

**For More Information**
If you have questions, please contact our Privacy Officer at:
- Phone: [Phone Number]
- Email: privacy@tshla.ai
- Hours: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM

We sincerely apologize for this incident and any inconvenience it may cause.

Sincerely,

[Privacy Officer Name]
Privacy Officer
TSHLA Medical
```

## Template 3: HHS Breach Report

**Use HHS Breach Portal:** https://ocrportal.hhs.gov/ocr/breach/wizard_breach.jsf

**Information Needed:**
1. Covered entity name and contact
2. Date of breach
3. Date discovered
4. Type of breach (hacking, unauthorized access, loss, theft, other)
5. Location of breached information (network server, email, paper, etc.)
6. Number of individuals affected
7. Business associate involved (if applicable)
8. Brief description

## Template 4: Business Associate Notification

```
[Date]

[Business Associate Name]
[Contact Person]
[Address]

RE: Notification of Security Incident

Dear [BA Contact]:

This letter serves as official notification that a security incident has occurred that may affect PHI for which you are a Business Associate under our BAA dated [date].

**Incident Details**
- Date of Incident: [date]
- Date Discovered: [date]
- Nature: [description]
- PHI Affected: [types and volume]

**Required Actions**
Per Section [X] of our BAA, you must:
1. Investigate the incident from your end
2. Provide us with a detailed report within [timeframe]
3. Implement corrective actions
4. Notify us of any additional affected individuals you identify

**Our Response**
We have:
- [List actions taken]
- Initiated breach notification process
- Reported to HHS (if applicable)

**Timeline**
We require your incident report by [date].

Please contact our Privacy Officer immediately at privacy@tshla.ai or [phone].

Sincerely,

[Privacy Officer Name]
[Title]
```

## Template 5: Annual Report to HHS (<500 individuals)

Submit via HHS Breach Portal by March 1 following calendar year.

**Required Information:**
- All breaches affecting fewer than 500 individuals
- Date of each breach
- Brief description of each breach
- Number of individuals affected by each

## Checklist: Notification Process

- [ ] Complete breach risk assessment
- [ ] Determine number of individuals affected
- [ ] Prepare individual notification letters
- [ ] Send individual notifications within 60 days
- [ ] Submit to HHS within 60 days (if 500+)
- [ ] Notify media if 500+ in jurisdiction
- [ ] Notify state AG if required
- [ ] Notify business associate if involved
- [ ] Document all notifications sent
- [ ] Maintain breach log
- [ ] File documentation (retain 6 years)
