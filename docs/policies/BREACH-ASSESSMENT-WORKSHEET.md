## Step 1: Was There Impermissible Use or Disclosure?

- [ ] YES - PHI was accessed, used, or disclosed in violation of Privacy Rule
- [ ] NO - Access was authorized → **NOT A BREACH**

## Step 2: Does an Exception Apply?

### Unintentional Access
- [ ] Access was by workforce member acting in good faith
- [ ] Access was within scope of authority
- [ ] Information was not further used or disclosed
→ If ALL checked: **NOT A BREACH**

### Inadvertent Disclosure
- [ ] Disclosure was to another authorized person at same organization
- [ ] Information was not further used or disclosed
→ If BOTH checked: **NOT A BREACH**

### Unable to Retain
- [ ] Good faith belief that unauthorized person could not have retained information
→ If checked: **NOT A BREACH**
