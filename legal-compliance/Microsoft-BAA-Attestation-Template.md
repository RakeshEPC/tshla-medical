### BAA Source and Authority

Per Microsoft's standard business practices, the HIPAA Business Associate Agreement is incorporated automatically through:

1. **Microsoft Product Terms** (formerly Online Services Terms)
   - Available at: https://www.microsoft.com/licensing/terms
   - Effective for all Azure customers who are HIPAA covered entities or business associates

2. **Microsoft Products and Services Data Protection Addendum (DPA)**
   - Incorporated by reference into our Azure subscription
   - Includes HIPAA-specific provisions in Appendix 3: "Security Measures"
   - Available at: https://www.microsoft.com/licensing/docs/view/Microsoft-Products-and-Services-Data-Protection-Addendum-DPA

3. **Azure Subscription Agreement**
